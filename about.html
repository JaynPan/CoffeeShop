FOUNDATION IN 2005 DOWNTOWN VANCOUVER

The Chill coffee is a place to come relax and meet new amazing people!
We dedicate ourselves to making this an environment that you feel safe and accepted in! We designed a special place for this in the quietest and most private part of our coffee shop, where you can enjoy pleasant relaxation and take care of your body.This is a place where you can forget work and stress, forget the chaos of the outside world and dedicate as much time as possible to the person you are closest to in the world.
